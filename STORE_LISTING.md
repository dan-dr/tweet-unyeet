# Chrome Web Store Listing — Tweet Unyeet

## Name (12/45 chars)

Tweet Unyeet

## Short description (89/132 chars)

Save tweets you've scrolled past on your X home timeline — stored locally, searchable, private.

## Category

Social & Communication

## Detailed description

Tweet Unyeet saves tweets you've actually scrolled past on your X home timeline, so you can find them again if they disappear from your feed.

**Key features**

- Saves tweets only after they enter your viewport on x.com/home
- Home timeline only — ignores individual tweet pages and replies
- Detects your timeline tabs (For You, Following, pinned lists) and lets you choose which to save from
- Shows which timeline each tweet was saved from
- Fuzzy search by author, tweet text, or timeline name
- Configurable retention limit (default: 100 tweets)

**How to use**

1. Install Tweet Unyeet and open [x.com/home](https://x.com/home)
2. Scroll your home timeline as usual
3. Open the extension popup to browse, search, and configure settings

**Privacy**

All saved tweets stay in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`. Nothing is sent to any server.
